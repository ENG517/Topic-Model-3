Using the Seed Maker

If you have played through a few seasons in the game you will gain access to the seed maker. This machine will allow you to create seeds from existing crops at no extra cost.

You must already have at least one fully grown version of your desired crop to turn into seeds.
  1. Equip one or more of the crop you wish to turn into seeds.
  2. Note:
    If you have a stack of crops equipped all of them will be converted into seeds.
    Right click to place them in the seed maker.
  3. Note:
    After a few moments a pop up will appear over the seed maker with an image of the crop seeds you just crafted. This means that all the seeds are ready to be collected.
    Left-click to collect finished seeds.
